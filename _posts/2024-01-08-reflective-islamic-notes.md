Writing about Islam requires care. A personal blog is not a place to pretend expertise or issue unsupported rulings. It can be a place to record learning, reflect on practice, and track questions for further study.

My rules for this category:

- Distinguish personal reflection from religious instruction.
- Add sources when discussing specific claims.
- Avoid speaking beyond what I understand.
- Leave questions open when they need qualified scholarship.
- Write with respect and humility.

This structure helps keep the notes useful without making them careless. If a post needs evidence, the source section should be filled before publishing.
