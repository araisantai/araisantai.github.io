Language learning works better when I separate exposure from review. I need enough input to meet real phrases, and enough repetition to keep useful words available.

The loop I want to maintain:

- Listen or read daily, even briefly.
- Save only the words that appear repeatedly or matter personally.
- Review vocabulary in small batches.
- Speak or write simple sentences before I feel ready.
- Notice mistakes without turning them into drama.

The goal is not to collect resources. The goal is to build contact with the language often enough that it becomes familiar.

Progress should be measured by usable phrases, listening comfort, and consistency.
